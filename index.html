FAQ's

Is UniPrep free to use?

Yes, UniPrep is completely free to use with no in-app purchases.

Does UniPrep have material for all engineering branches?

Yes, UniPrep has all engineering branches with all possible notes, pyqs and other material for the available branches.

Can I download notes and other material on my device?

Yes, you can download any PDF file (excluding notes) by accessing the same on your google drive account.

Is there any kind of authentication required?

No, UniPrep does not ask for any type of authentication from our user.
Note: Signup is only used for subject filteration.

Is UniPrep available for iOS?

Unfortunetly UniPrep is currently unavailable for iOS devices. Our team is already working to bring an alternate solution for all the non-android users as well!

ABOUT

UniPrep is an all in one app for the engineers. Bringing easy access to study material such as notes, pyqs, syllabus, time table and much more without any charges and an ad-free experience.
